Binary Game of Life

 

To get started, click the Run button below, or interact with the grid below to fill in cells. Binary life works the same as normal life, except when new cells are born, they take on the color that the majority of their parent cells have. Each team begins below with the acorn pattern. Refresh the page to get the acorn pattern back.

If you're not sure what all of this is about, check out the Wikipedia article on Conway's Game of Life.
